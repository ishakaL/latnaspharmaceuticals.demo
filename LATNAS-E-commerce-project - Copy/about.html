About Lathes Pharmaceuticals

Lathes Pharmaceuticals Nig. Ltd is a leading provider of quality pharmaceutical products in Nigeria. With over 30 outlets and a wide distribution network, we are committed to delivering safe, effective, and affordable medications to communities across the country.

Our mission is to improve health outcomes by offering accessible pharmaceutical solutions and maintaining the highest standards in product integrity and customer service.

We collaborate with top manufacturers and ensure all our products are certified by the appropriate health regulatory agencies.
